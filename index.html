About Me


Hi, I'm Aly Pilkons. I'm a former teacher turned front-end web developer. I have a BA in liberal arts from Hampshire College where I studied creative writing and visual art, and I love bringing my art background into my web development process and creating beautiful, responsive, user-friendly sites and apps.









Skills


In April 2016 I completed FreeCodeCamp's 400 hour front-end development curriculum and earned my Front End Development Certification.

Currently, I am proficient in HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, Javascript, jQuery, and AJAX, as well as in version control using Git/GitHub.
