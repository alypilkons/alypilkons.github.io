Hi, I'm Aly


I'm a former teacher turned front-end web developer. I have a BA in liberal arts from Hampshire College where I studied creative writing and visual art, and I love bringing my art background into my web development process and creating beautiful, responsive, user-friendly sites and apps.



My Skillset


HTML5 CSS3 JavaScript jQuery AJAX Git/GitHub


I am a self-taught developer constantly working to improve my current skills and learn new frameworks, libraries, and programming languages. I’ve utilized a variety of resources in my education, including completing FreeCodeCamp’s 400 hour front-end development curriculum and earning their Front End Development Certificate.


Along with the above technologies, I also have experience using media queries and front-end frameworks like Bootstrap to create responsive designs that look great on all screen sizes. Currently, I am learning to build interfaces with React and develop games with Phaser.
